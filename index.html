Maggie L. Walker Governor’s School (MLWGS)

Maggie L. Walker Governor’s School for Government and International Studies, known as MLWGS, is a public regional magnet high school in Richmond, Virginia. The school provides a comprehensive educational program designed to advance gifted students’ understanding. It also prides itself on fostering a diverse and supportive community that cultivates student character and their ability to contribute, collaborate, and lead.
